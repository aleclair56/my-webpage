A little bit about me.

My Journey to becoming a chef was a bit unorthodox. I studied business at the University at Buffalo, however discovered my love for Kitchens shortly after Graduation. The culture of the "Back of House" was intoxicating and I grew hungry to learn more. I quickly moved up the ranks becoming a sous chef at 24, and eventually an executive chef at 27. I continue to scour the world for new and innovatve dishes that I can put my twist on!

Cousin Alex's Salmon

Featured at Marcus Samuelson's Red Rooster, this dish is layered with complex flavors. It is served with lager fermented tomato curry, smoked baby carrots, yucca, and pistacio pesto.

Pan Seared Skate Wing

This delicate fish combines perfectly with a rich garlic, curry puree. it is served with Caperberries, Calabrian chiles and topped with a corn, tomato and bok Choy succotach for a fresh crunch.

Autumn Pumpkin Soup

an Autumn classic, this rich soup will make any day taste like the holidays. it is topped with spiced pepitas for texture, a chantilly cream, and fresh herbs.
